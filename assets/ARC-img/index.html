Overview

Before the breakthrough of automation, and even after that, policy checking has been a manual process. If you are familiar with the task, you must also know how much time and effort this process consumes. A regular policy document or binder consists of around 30 to 250 pages and even more! Going through the exhaustive policy documents, verifying their details with the management systems, and then manually taking notes of differences or matching information to create a checklist sounds quite intimidating. You can only imagine doing it on repeat for each policy !

Do you know the entire policy check process takes up to 2 hours for each policy? And commercial insurance companies are still following the same old, tedious manual review of policy documents.

However, you can reduce this time to only less than 3 minutes and your answer is Policy Check Automation.

Methodology

Tech Tammina utilizes its Appian expertise for policy check automation solutions. We have utilized the two big features AI and Bots, of Appian that helped us build this robust and time-efficient solution for commercial insurance carriers.

Appian IDP

Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) has benefited Insurance Agencies / Brokers & Carriers with faster document processing. It has also improved the accuracy of extraction and productivity, and ensured paperless document processing. In addition, since no manual data entry is required with IDP, there are no more human errors, leading the solution to be cost-efficient.

With the help of Appian IDP's AI - Auto ML capabilities, we can analyze and extract relevant data points from any Policy Document irrespective of various formats / patterns.

Appian RPA

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is transforming the way insurance organizations operate by streamlining their manual processes and reducing the burden on human employees. Appian RPA communicates with legacy business systems and applications to increase productivity by leveraging a scalable, digital workforce.

Using Appian RPA we can accurately extract data from any agency management system based on Policy Number / Type / Customer Name.

Appian Low-Code

Appian's Low-code capabilities let us rapidly design and build beautiful UIs with quicker development deployments, and integrate all your technologies, data, and systems into a single workflow to accelerate business results. Leveraging such capabilities, we have developed a pre-defined customizable checklist to showcase the difference and matching information consolidated from Appian IDP & Appian RPA.

We were impressed by the platform's capabilities and its capacity to quickly and precisely collect and synthesise the data we required for Policy Check. The application's key feature is its flexibility, which enables highly customizable workflows to meet our daily business needs and dramatically shortens process times.
